About Us

It's A Breeze Designs is a small shop that packs a lot of punch! Don't let our size fool you, we put a lot of care and love into each and every design made. All of our designs aim to turn your special occasions into elegant and beautiful memories. It's a Breeze takes pride in making each design, well.. a breeze to make! We're located in the Washington D.C. area, so meeting up with clients is easy breezy. If you're interested in collaborating please see our Contact Page to message us directly.

Designs made with Love

Our tiny boutique was inspired to grow from all happy occasions of life! There's definitely something to be said about a kind card and message special to someone you love. Our designs are inspired by moments that make us laugh, cry and in general spark emotion within us and those around us. Whether it's a birthday, wedding or a special reunion with friends and family, each design is crafted with lots of thought, vision and heart. If you've got a special moment you want to create, we can help.
