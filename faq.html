Frequently Asked Questions

Here is a compiled list of frequently asked questions from lovely clients of ours. Please review below before reaching out and asking. If your question isn't here or you are interersted in custom designs, please drop us a line and we'll be super happy to help!

How do your products work? Are they physical or download?

All of my products are currently downloadable only.

Where else can I see more of your products?

My Etsy Page! Please refer to that link through the Navigation Menu.

Can I find your products anywhere else besides Etsy?

For now, Etsy is the only available store that you can shop for our products. However, we are looking to expand in the future!

Am I able to make any additional edits or modifications to current projects?

Current projects are sold as is - if you’d like to make edits to an existing product we can work it as a custom job with an additional fee to the project’s original price.

I am interested in a complete custom job! How do I go about working with you on it?

Please refer to my contact page to message me for a quote. I'd be happy to talk to you about your new upcoming project and how we can collaborate on it! Contact and messaging is also available on my Etsy page.

How much are custom jobs?

Custom jobs are priced separately on a case by case base.

Would you be able to print out physical products and ship to me?

Currently I am only available for downloaded designs.

I don't like the colors on this design! Can I change colors or text to a current project?

Yes! There is a small additional fee, but I can update the design as a modified custom product.

I have additional questions not listed here, how can I contact you?

Definitely feel free to ask me questions via the contact page or contact me directly at itsabreezedesigns@gmail.com.
